With 12 years of experience in film wedding photography Melbourne and wedding photography Melbourne, Candidly Yours – by Simon delivers candid, film-like, and raw photography that feels as genuine as the moments themselves. As a professional wedding photographer Melbourne, my relaxed, non-intrusive approach allows me to blend seamlessly into your wedding day, capturing heartfelt emotions and the little details that make it uniquely yours.

Specializing in film wedding photography Melbourne, I focus on authentic, unposed moments—from spontaneous laughter to quiet, tender moments. Plus, I offer a 24-hour turnaround for key moment shots—because your story deserves to be relived as soon as possible.
